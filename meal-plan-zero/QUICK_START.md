# Meal Plan Zero - Quick Start Guide for New Enhancements

## What's New?

Your game has been significantly enhanced with 5 major gameplay improvements:

### 1. **Investigation State** - Workers now actively search your last-known position
   - When suspicion gets high, workers move to where they last saw you and wait
   - Look for "?!" above their heads to identify investigating workers
   - Strategic advantage: Hide while they search, then move to a new location

### 2. **Risk vs Reward Food** - High-value items are now genuinely risky
   - **200 points**: Pizza in kitchen (near Chef Kim - VERY risky!)
   - **150 points**: Pizza in upper dining area (risky)
   - **50-60 points**: Corner items like muffins/apples (safe)
   - **30 points**: Coffee (low value, safe)
   - **Strategy**: Balance grabbing high-points vs staying alive

### 3. **Noise Matters** - Running is now LOUD and DANGEROUS
   - Sprinting creates visible noise radius (red circle when detected)
   - Very loud running near a worker → **instant alert**
   - Crouching is silent and safe
   - **Strategy**: Crouch when workers are nearby, run in clear zones

### 4. **Worker Coordination** - Multiple workers are deadlier together
   - When 2+ workers are alert/chasing/investigating → detection rises faster
   - Once spotted, it's much harder to escape
   - **Strategy**: Avoid detection in first place, or use hiding spots to break pursuit

### 5. **Better AI** - Workers smell danger faster
   - Loud sounds trigger faster suspicion
   - Investigation areas show worker intent
   - Workers remember your last position

## Tips for Success

### ✅ DO:
- ✓ **Crouch** when workers are nearby - silent and gives time to hide
- ✓ **Use hiding spots** (lockers/closets) to break detection chains
- ✓ **Plan routes** - know where workers patrol before moving
- ✓ **Mix strategies** - grab some easy safe food, plus ONE risky item
- ✓ **Use distractions** (Q) to lure workers away from exits
- ✓ **Watch detection meter** - when it spikes, hide immediately

### ❌ DON'T:
- ✗ **Don't run past workers** - noise triggers instant alerts now
- ✗ **Don't get greedy** - grabbing 3 items near one worker = caught
- ✗ **Don't ignore suspicious workers** - they're about to go alert
- ✗ **Don't fight multiple workers** - coordination bonus makes them deadly
- ✗ **Don't waste distractions** - use them strategically to create gaps

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Detection keeps rising even when I hide?**
A: Other workers might see you. Make sure you're in a proper hiding spot (locker/closet) that breaks line of sight.

**Q: Why do workers suddenly go alert?**
A: You might be making noise near them, or another worker triggered them. Check for multiple workers coordinating.

**Q: Can I outrun workers?**
A: Not really - they're too fast. Focus on hiding or using distractions instead.

**Q: What's the best strategy?**
A: Mix it! Grab 2-3 safe items + 1 risky item for a good score without too much danger.
